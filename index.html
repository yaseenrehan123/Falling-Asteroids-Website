What is Falling Asteroids ?

Now, you might be curious what is this game about? It's a simple Javascript game where you have to destroy the asteroids coming from above, sounds simple right ?

It's mostly designed for mobile since at pc it will be hard to click multiple asteroids at once, but nonetheless you can still give it a try if you have touch input on pc.

Keypoints:

  • Can work in both portrait or landscape mode
  • Is fully responsive
  • difficulty automatically scales with time
  • You have maximum 10 lives
